## What is Adobe Experience Platform?

Adobe Experience Platform (AEP) is the industry's most powerful open data 
platform built for customer experience management.

## Real-Time Customer Profile

The core of AEP is its ability to create unified customer profiles in real time.

### How Profiles Are Built

1. Data is ingested from multiple sources
2. Identity stitching merges records
3. A unified profile is created and updated in real time

## Key Concepts

### Schemas and XDM
Experience Data Model (XDM) is the standardized framework used to describe 
customer experience data.

### Datasets
Datasets are containers for data that conform to a specific schema.

### Segments
Segments allow you to define audiences based on profile attributes and 
behavioral data.

## Real-World Use Case

Imagine a customer visits your website, then your mobile app, then calls 
support. AEP stitches all three touchpoints into one unified profile, 
enabling truly personalized experiences.

## Conclusion

AEP's Real-Time CDP is a powerful platform for organizations serious about 
customer data unification and activation.
